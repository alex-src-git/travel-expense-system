# Specification

## The Travel Expense System will need to include the following inputs and outputs:  
### Inputs   
- Number of journeys completed
- What the claim is for (travel and expenses or just travel)
- Cost for the travel and expenses
### Outputs  
- Receipt details
- Total cost of the expenses combined
- Tax that can reclaimed by the company, calculated at 20% of the total cost
- Amount that will need to be paid to the employee
- They will be awarded 100% of the travel cost
- Employees can only claim up to £50 of expenses, anything over will need to be covered by the employee
- The non-refundable amount
- The average payment required
- The largest payment made
